# BridGenta Constitutional Governance

## Purpose

These constitutional documents establish the principles that guide software engineering, project documentation, communication, storytelling, explainability, and AI collaboration within the BridGenta project. They exist to ensure architectural consistency, quality, and clarity regardless of which contributor (human or AI) performs the work.

---

## Guiding Principle

> The constitutional documents are the single source of truth for engineering philosophy, communication standards, and project presentation within BridGenta.

---

## Who Must Follow These Documents

These rules are mandatory and apply to all contributors:
- Frank Duru
- Human contributors
- AI agents / coding assistants
- External collaborators
- Future maintainers

---

## When They Must Be Read

Every contributor must review the applicable constitutional documents before modifying:
- The homepage
- Project details and case studies pages
- The about page
- Documentation files
- Case study metadata or assets
- Engineering explanations
- AI-generated copy and code integrations
- Architectural descriptions

---

---

## AI Collaboration Workflow

Every AI-assisted contribution must follow this mandatory sequence:

1. **Read**: Review all applicable constitutional documents in this directory.
2. **Validate**: Validate the requested work against the constitutions.
3. **Identify**: Identify and document any constitutional conflicts.
4. **Implement**: Perform the requested implementation.
5. **Report**: Produce a Constitutional Compliance Report summarizing how the work aligns with the rules.
6. **Highlight**: Explicitly highlight any assumptions made or deviations required.

---

## Human Review

Constitutional compliance does not replace engineering judgement. Final responsibility for code quality, security, and architectural alignment always remains with the human reviewer.

---

## Future Evolution

BridGenta's constitutions are living engineering documents. They may evolve over time to adapt to new technologies and processes while always preserving structural consistency and long-term maintainability.

---

## Closing Statement

> BridGenta applies constitutional thinking not only to software engineering, but also to documentation, communication, and AI collaboration. Every contribution should strengthen clarity, explainability, consistency, and long-term trust.
